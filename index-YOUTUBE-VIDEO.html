A Legacy
of Glamour

• Established early year 2000 when Mr. Salam Markos and Mr. Anwar Zaydan From Lebanon translated both their names to Italian then Pace E Luce was born.

• Mr. Salam and Mr. Anwar both hairdressers always dreamt on building one destination that take care of all women and men beauty needs

• From a small in their home town to the largest group of saloons in the middle east and number one celebrity destination employing over 500 hairdressers in the region.

• The story didn't end here, after the large success in the middle east the decision was made and the business plan begins: Europe, United States of America and Australia and soon in London to take their extensive expertise in the hair and beauty industry to the rest of the world.
